History


There was once a studious boy in a town. He completed his intermediate in first class and wanted to do computers in graduation but his poor parents could not afford for his studies. The boy was forced to put an end to his dream of studying. But the boy's mother did not give up her attempts and desperately wanted for help from anyone of their acquaintance. One of relatives understood the boy's zeal and helped him to continue his studies. The boy fared well in his studies and now he is a successful software engineer.   Having understood the pain and trauma what he went through, the engineer set his aspirations focused on providing education and financial support to the meritorious poor students. If only, there were wishes allover to make this world a brighter place. How wonderful it is that nobody needs to wait a single moment before starting to improve the world.
