Ongoing Activities


  • Adopted a merit student, who is studying B.Tech in Vizianagaram. We are helping out to pay her college fees for third year. If she continued to perform well in coming year, she will get scholarship for next year (fourth year) also.
  • Planned to donate school uniforms for 7th and 8th class government school students on this independence day August 15th 2017.

Past Accomplishments


  • Four of our students got jobs in corporate organizations
  • 70+ students took help from our foundation to achieve next level in their career
  • Adopted two blind students and helping them to grow in their academic career
  • Adopted two government schools in villages
  • Adopted Donkada village to provide scholarships for students
  • Donated text books for 10th class students (150+)
  • Provided Mobile science lab facilities for two government schools
  • Raised funds to provide basic kitchen needs for Pallavi Old age home
  • Conducted FREE Eye checkup camps for two government schools
  • Provided medicines and spectacles for students who have eye sight problems
  • Provided school uniforms for 9th and 10th class government high school students (150+)
  • 600+ members contributed to this success with their financial and moral support
  • An article published in “The Better India” website about our foundation activities
  • http://www.thebetterindia.com/2288/well-wishers-foundation-enabling-education-for-adults/
